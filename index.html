One Story. One Chapter.

FBLA is national program that brings students together to compete in region, state, and national regions. We are proud to have a FBLA chapter at Cross Keys High School because we value all of our students skills and career goals. FBLA helps prepare a students ability in public speaking, competions, and getting real experience in the business world. If you are interested in joining FBLA, please contact our advisor, Mr. Green located in room 201.
